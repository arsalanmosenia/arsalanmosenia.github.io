Bio

Arsalan Mosenia is currently a postdoctoral researcher, jointly working with Profs. Mung Chiang (Purdue University) and Prateek Mittal (Princeton University).

He is interested in the design and development of privacy-preserving and secure systems. His research lies at the intersection of Security & Privacy, Machine Learning (ML), and Fog/Edge Computing. His Ph.D. research focused on emerging security and privacy challenges in the Internet-of-Things (IoT) paradigm. As a postdoctoral researcher, he is exploring adversarial ML and the security of autonomous vehicles (AVs).
